ABOUT US

Vision and Mission

We envision a world where communities are better equipped to handle disasters and emergencies through advanced crowd monitoring and management solutions. By harnessing the power of deep learning and drone technology, we aim to mitigate the impact of disasters and save lives.

Our mission is to develop a robust system that leverages deep learning techniques, particularly YOLO v8, to accurately detect and track individuals in disaster areas. By analyzing thermal images captured by drones, we can provide real-time insights into crowd dynamics, injury detection, and resource allocation.
